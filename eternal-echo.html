What's Inside

High-quality MIDI files ready to use in your productions

"Eternal Echo" captures the emotional vulnerability and raw delivery that characterizes MGK's style in "Twin Flame." This sample pack includes carefully crafted MIDI files that you can use with your favorite virtual instruments to create emotional, guitar-driven tracks.

The pack follows a structure that builds from sparse, intimate moments to emotional peaks, giving you the perfect foundation for creating tracks with depth and feeling.

Features:

  • 4 high-quality MIDI files (melody, drums, bass, guitar)
  • 75 BPM tempo for an emotional, intimate feel
  • Compatible with all major DAWs
  • Royalty-free for both personal and commercial use

How to Use

Get the most out of your Eternal Echo sample pack

1

Import MIDI Files

Import the MIDI files into your preferred Digital Audio Workstation (DAW) such as FL Studio, Ableton Live, Logic Pro, or any other DAW that supports MIDI.

2

Assign Virtual Instruments

Assign appropriate virtual instruments to each MIDI track:

  • Guitar VST for the guitar.mid file
  • Bass VST for the bass.mid file
  • Drum kit VST for the drums.mid file
  • Piano or synth VST for the melody.mid file

3

Add Effects

Enhance your sound with atmospheric effects like reverb and delay to achieve that emotional MGK vibe. Consider adding a heartbeat sound effect in the outro for emotional impact.

4

Make It Your Own

Feel free to modify the MIDI files to fit your creative vision. Add your own vocals, change the instruments, or rearrange the structure to create something unique.
