How to Use

Get the most out of your Super Trap Loop Kit

1

Import Audio Files

Import the audio files into your preferred Digital Audio Workstation (DAW) such as FL Studio, Ableton Live, Logic Pro, or any other DAW that supports audio files.

2

Arrange Your Beat

Drag and drop the melody loops into your arrangement. These loops are designed to be the foundation of your track, so start by arranging them to create your song structure.

3

Add Your Elements

Layer your own drums, bass, and additional elements to complete your beat. The melody loops are designed to give you plenty of space to add your own creative touch.

4

Make It Your Own

Feel free to process the loops with effects, chop them up, or pitch them to create something unique. These loops are royalty-free, so you can use them in your commercial releases.
