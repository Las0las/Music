Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our subscription plans

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account dashboard. Go to "Subscription" and click "Cancel Subscription." Your subscription will remain active until the end of your current billing period.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account dashboard. If you upgrade, you'll be charged the prorated difference for the remainder of your billing cycle. If you downgrade, the change will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Are the samples royalty-free?

All samples in our Basic and Pro plans are royalty-free for personal use. For commercial use (monetized content, client work, etc.), you'll need the Studio plan which includes a commercial license for all samples.

How often are new samples added?

We add new sample packs every week. Pro and Studio members get early access to new releases, while Basic members get access when they're officially released.

What formats are the samples available in?

Our samples are available in WAV, MIDI, and sometimes AIFF formats. All audio samples are high-quality 24-bit/44.1kHz WAV files, ensuring the best sound quality for your productions.
